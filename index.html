The Philosophy: Algorithmic Bandwidth

Optimizing the Zone of Proximal Development (ZPD) through adaptive systems. ThresholdEd converts the ZPD into a dynamic bandwidth to keep every student at their individual upper boundary—the "Sweet Spot" of learning.

  • Adaptive Learning: Skill-based mastery adjusted to the ZPD.
  • Data-Driven Success: Technology-enhanced data collection to automate meaningful practice.
  • Linguistic Equity: Assessing developmental readiness, not just language proficiency.
  • AI-Automated Scaffolding: Reducing mental load to encourage skill automatization.
